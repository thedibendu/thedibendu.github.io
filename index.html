About

Decoding the
machines of life.

I am a Ph.D. student working on ATP-driven molecular motors involved in DNA translocation and viral genome packaging. My research combines protein biochemistry, cryo-electron microscopy, computational image analysis, and model building to understand how protein machines generate force and move DNA at the molecular scale.

I am especially interested in computational approaches for cryo-EM structure determination — including model fitting into electron density maps, workflow automation, and Python-based analysis.

Molecular Motors

Viral DNA Packaging

Computational Workflows
